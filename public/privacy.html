Velocity App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Velocity ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

Location Data: Velocity actively collects background location data to map your cycling and motorcycle rides, calculate real-time speeds, and show your distance to other group members. This location data is ONLY collected when you explicitly start a ride and remains active in the background until you press "End Ride".

Audio Data: We access your device microphone for the Walkie-Talkie feature. Audio data is streamed temporarily and is NEVER recorded or stored on our servers.

Account Info: We securely collect your email and name through Supabase authentication for profile generation.

3. Data Security and Encryption

All sensitive data (e.g., location coordinates, messages) transmitted between the App and our backend servers is encrypted in transit using strict SSL/HTTPS protocols. We employ Row Level Security (RLS) on our databases to ensure unauthorized users cannot access your private riding data.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your data. We use Supabase (Authentication/Database) and Firebase Cloud Messaging (Push Notifications) as our infrastructural providers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about your privacy, please contact us at privacy@velocity.com
